Sunday Soccer Practice Plan

Date: Sunday, 11/09/25

Time: 8:30 – 10:00 AM

Location: Under Armor Field

Focus: One-touch speed, passing accuracy, decision-making, and finishing.

8:30 – 8:40 AM — Warm-Up (10 min)

  • Light jog around the field (2 laps)
  • Dynamic stretching (legs, hips, shoulders)
  • Agility steps (ladder or cones)

8:40 – 9:05 AM — Improve Speed, Agility & Power (25 min)

Drill: Improve Speed, Agility & Power

  • Explosive movement patterns
  • Acceleration + deceleration work
  • Directional changes under pressure
  • Footwork + power generation

9:05 – 9:25 AM — Passing & First Touch (20 min)

Drill: Tiki Pass

Focus on clean passes and first touch.

9:25 – 9:40 AM — Quick Combination Drill (15 min)

Drill: Quick Pass Drill

9:40 – 9:58 AM — Shooting & Finishing (18 min)

Drill: Crossing and Finishing

  • Winger serves a cross
  • Two attackers time their runs
  • Rotate roles
  • Goalkeepers: angles + reaction

Focus points: timing of the run, laces vs. placement, communication between crossers & strikers.

9:58 – 10:00 AM — Cool-Down & Wrap-Up (2 min)

  • Light jog
  • Static stretch
  • Team talk: effort, teamwork, next steps
